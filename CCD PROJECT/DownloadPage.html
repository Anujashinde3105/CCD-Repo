FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT THE CCD
APP AND HOW TO USE THE EXISTING BEANS
FROM THE CCD APP FROM HEREON

Why is the CCD App getting disabled?

We need to improve the functionalities of the app and make it more exciting for our users, therefore
the app is going to be removed temporarily.

When is the CCD App going to be back?

CCD App will be back soon with all new features. Timeline for the relaunch will be communicated
soon.

Will we be able to order online from CCD without the app?

Yes, you can order online through our new contactless platform - www.ccdonline.in and have a
seamless experience.

Can we order takeaway from ccdonline.in as we used to using PICKUP & GO feature on the CCD
app?

Yes, you can order takeaway using the PICKUP & GO option through www.ccdonline.in and even
schedule your pickups for a particular time of the day.

What will happen to the Beans I earned through the CCD app?

Your Beans are safe and will be transferred to your ccdonline.in account if you register using the
same mobile number as used for the app.

I could not use my beans during the lockdown period. Would they have expired?

Considering the Covid-19 lockdown, we have already extended the expiry date of all existing Beans
till 31st December 2020. All your Beans are safe and valid till then. You can also choose to use them
on www.ccdonline.in.

What will be the validity of the Beans now?

You can use your existing Beans from the app till 31st December 2020.

How can I check and use my App Beans on www.ccdonline.in portal?

You just need to log in to the ccdonline.in portal using the same mobile number as that used on the
app. Then follow the below mentioned steps -


Users With Existing Beans: (FOR 1st time ccdonline users)


  • Visit www.ccdonline.in
  • Select café or enter the seat code mentioned on the tables
  • Select products from the menu and add them to your cart
  • Proceed to pay, enter the phone number as used on the app and you will receive an OTP
  • On verifying the phone number you will get to see your App Beans on the homepage next to
    the profile icon on the top right corner (only if same number was provided in the app)
  • You will get to see your balance Beans and will also be allowed to use them at the checkout
    page based on the applicable terms & conditions

Users With Existing Beans: (FOR existing ccdonline users)


  • Visit www.ccdonline.in
  • Select café or enter the seat code mentioned on the tables
  • Once you are logged in you will get to see your App Beans on the homepage next to the profile
    icon on the top right corner (only if same number was provided in the app)
  • You will get to see your balance Beans and will also be allowed to use them at the checkout
    page based on the applicable terms & conditions.

Introducing...Beans! Get the benefits by visiting www.ccdonline.in

What is a Bean?


  • Beans are the reward points you earn whenever you make a food or beverage purchase from
    our stores using the www.ccdonline.in portal
  • You get up to 7% Beans as cash back on every spend on food and/or beverages
  • On redemption, the value of 1 Bean = Re. 1
  • Beans are earned on the base price of the item after discount, before tax and less Beans
    redeemed during the transaction.

Earning Beans

You earn beans every time you use ccdonline.in portal to place a food or beverage order


  • Earn up to 7% cashback on the transaction value (excluding beans redeemed, discounts &
    taxes). 7% Beans earning will be rounded down to the nearest whole number.

Redemption of Beans

You can redeem your earned Beans against any beverage purchase (excluding Beverages available
under the “totally Worth it menu’, combos and packaged beverages) at any CCD store (excluding
CCD Express and CCD Airport Cafes).

.The ccdonline.in portal is applicable across vast network of cafes. However, from time-to-time, there
will be select cafes which will be exempt for a temporary period from Beans redemption due to
technical constraints / changes.

Beans are redeemed only against non-combos and non-packaged beverages, on your final billed
value after discount and after tax. However Beans cannot be redeemed against Beverages available
under the “Totally Worth It” menu.

1 Bean is equivalent to Re. 1.

Do Beans have an expiry date?

Purchased Beans and Beans earned on transactions have a validity of 180 days from the day of
earning, post which they will expire.

For example, if you have earned Beans through transactions on 15th August, 2020, they will expire
on 11th February, 2021 (12:00 AM).

What can I purchase with my accumulated Beans?

You can use your accrued Beans to purchase Beverages (excluding Beverages available under the
“Totally Worth It” menu, combos and Packaged Beverages) from our CCD
stores (excluding CCD Express and CCD Airport Cafes.)

Why am I not able to redeem my Beans?


  • Transaction Beans have an expiry period of 180 days. Don't ignore us for loo long!
  • There is no Internet connectivity on your mobile device.
  • Beans Redemption is not possible for "Cash on Delivery" orders.

Why am I not able to purchase Beans now?

We have temporarily disabled the feature of purchase Beans on the portal in order to facilitate
certain technical changes. But, we will be back soon. Stay tuned.

Can I transfer my accrued Beans to my friends?

Not yet, but we are working on it.

Can I encash my accrued/purchased Beans?

No, you can't cash the Beans. You can use them to pay for your CCD purchases.

Do I earn Beans while redeeming Beans?

Beans are earned only on the amount paid through the online payments or by cash/card at the
counter. The amount of Beans redeemed will not earn any Beans.

Can I make a partial payment for my bill using Beans/Online or Cash or card?


  • Sure! You can opt for paying with Beans/cash/card or combine all of these options to make
    a payment for your purchases.
  • If you opt to pay by combining all the available payment modes, Beans will be deducted first
    and any balance amount will be finally settled through cash/card.

Can I use my Beans while redeeming any offer?

Yes, you can use your Beans while redeeming any of the existing offers but you will not earn any
Beans while using such offers.

Do Beans earnings apply on subscription offers and normal offers at www.ccdonline.in ?

No, Beans cannot be earned for purchase of coffee subscriptions or any other offers

Can I earn or utilise Beans for ‘Cash on Delivery’ orders?

No, Beans cannot be earned or redeemed for ‘Cash on Delivery’ mode of orders.
